Objectives

Professional Development

Key Activities:


• Organize quarterly training workshops on teaching methodologies, digital literacy, and professional growth.
• Provide mentorship programs connecting experienced educators with younger teachers.
• Facilitate annual career development sessions in partnership with education stakeholders.

Outcomes:


• Enhanced teaching skills and digital literacy among female educators.
• Strong mentorship networks benefiting young and upcoming teachers.
• Increased career progression opportunities for female teachers.

Leadership & Governance

Key Activities:


• Conduct leadership training programs to equip teachers with governance skills.
• Encourage and support female educators to take up leadership positions in schools and professional associations.
• Host an annual leadership forum featuring women leaders in education and governance.

Outcomes:


• Increased representation of female teachers in school leadership roles.
• Strengthened advocacy for policies that promote women’s leadership in education.
• Development of confident, well-equipped female education leaders.

Gender & Social Equity

Key Activities:


• Organize awareness campaigns and policy dialogues on gender equity in education.
• Provide support platforms for GBV survivors, including referral services and counseling.
• Develop and advocate for gender-sensitive policies in schools and communities

Outcomes:


• Improved awareness and response to gender-based violence within schools.
• Implementation of gender-sensitive policies in educational institutions.
• Strengthened support systems for women facing social and gender challenges.

Financial Literacy & Economic Empowerment

Key Activities:


• Offer financial literacy workshops on budgeting, savings, and responsible borrowing
• Facilitate entrepreneurial skills training for female educators.
• Provide access to investment and financial planning resources in partnership with financial institutions.

Outcomes:


• Improved financial decision-making among female educators
• Increased economic independence through entrepreneurial activities.
• Strengthened financial stability and security for female teachers.

Community Engagement

Key Activities:


• Strengthen networks and partnerships with like-minded organizations.
• Organize mentorship programs for students to promote career guidance and life-skills development.
• Engage in community-based projects that promote education and social development.

Outcomes:


• Increased teacher-student engagement beyond the classroom.
• Strengthened collaborations between teachers and the wider community.
• Improved educational opportunities for students through teacher-led initiatives.

Mental Wellness

Key Activities:


• Conduct mental health awareness sessions for teachers.
• Provide stress management and wellness programs tailored for educators.
• Create a peer support network for emotional and psychological well-being.

Outcomes:


• Improved mental well-being among female educators.
• Increased teacher productivity and job satisfaction.
• Strengthened peer support systems to combat stress and burnout.

Advocacy & Promotion of Food & Nutrition Security & Blue Economy

Key Activities:


• Organize training sessions and awareness campaigns on food security and sustainable nutrition for schools and communities.
• Advocate for the integration of school gardening and feeding programs that promote balanced nutrition.
• Promote blue economy initiatives, including sustainable fishing, aquaculture, and water conservation education in schools.
• Develop strategic partnerships with agricultural and environmental organizations to enhance sustainable food production and nutrition education.

Outcomes:


• Increased awareness and adoption of sustainable nutrition practices in schools.
• Strengthened school feeding programs for better student health.
• Improved engagement of teachers in environmental sustainability efforts.

Inclusion of Teachers with Disabilities & Special Needs Education

Key Activities:


• Promote inclusivity in She4She TN activities to ensure full participation of teachers with disabilities.
• Organize capacity-building sessions for teachers on handling learners with special needs.
• Advocate for inclusive policies in schools that support both teachers and learners with disabilities.
• Develop partnerships with organizations that support special needs education to enhance teacher capacity and resource availability.

Outcomes:


• Increased participation of teachers with disabilities in professional development activities.
• Improved inclusivity and support for learners with disabilities.
• Strengthened policies for special needs education within schools.
